Privacy Policy

By using this site (Bomberz.io) you declare that you understand and agree with the following Privacy Policy.

What we collect

We will collect your IP Address, Email Adress, Username, and Password if they are supplied. The data will be stored to give you access to an Account and the privileges/features that come with one. Accounts are not required. If you do not create an Account, we will not store your IP Address, Email Address, Username, or Password.

How we store data

When we store data, we store it with the most secure methods that will work with our systems. We hash all IPs and Passwords supplied to us using the SHA-256 algorithm. Only you will know what your password is. Email Addresses are encrypted using the AES-256 standard.

Modification of data

We allow you to modify your data. You can do so in your Account Settings (you must be signed in). You can change your Password and Email Address. Your Username is permanent unless specific permission is granted. To change your Username, you may contact us.

Our commitments

We are committed to your privacy. We will never sell what little data we do store (Email Addresses, IP Addresses, Passwords, & Usernames) to anyone. We store the data on a server with restricted access, not just anyone on the team can access it. Likewise, all the data files are in a non-readable format.

About Bomberz.io

Bomberz.io is a fast-paced, 5v5, 2D shooter game inspired by CS:GO and Valorant. The goals are simple: diffuse the bomb planted by the opposing team or defend the bomb your team has planted.
